FX Corporate

FX Corporate

A solution that helps you deliver an outstanding FX trading experience to your corporate customers.

 

Corporate treasurers, payments clerks and other accounting staff from small and medium enterprises to larger corporations need to manage their company’s FX exposure. Conventional FX trading screens designed for professional or retail traders are not a great fit for this requirement. Instead, providing a user experience that supports real corporate treasurer workflows can be vital to retaining existing clients and attracting new ones. It also makes clients much more likely to migrate from high-cost voice channels to low-cost online trading.

 

Corporate treasurers of all sizes are looking to be more pro-active in how they manage their FX hedging, cash flows and payment activities. To effectively service the corporate market, banks need to provide better on-line trading solutions that address the ‘workflow’ challenges of corporate customers, whilst meeting best-practices of corporate governance around security, accountability and segregation of duties. This presents numerous challenges for silo based banks. This is because their traditional trading platforms are more suited for professional institutional clients rather than risk adverse corporate treasurers, and many struggle to offer access to the relevant back end systems to enable clients to seamlessly hedge exposures, manage cash flows and make payments through a single front end platform. Caplin’s FX Corporate solution addresses these challenges, through a suite of ‘ready to use’ treasury dashboards, delivering the broad range of treasury services required from across the bank. The solution offers a workflow-based dashboard focused on specific aspects of corporate treasury activity such as: managing upcoming maturities, trade execution, uploading block trades, forward strips and allocations, with post-trade services managing settlement instructions. On-boarding workflows support the assigning of role based permissioning to users, such as a Payment clerk/arranger who can set up transactions. When they are ready, the Treasurer/approver is notified by email to go online and authorise/amend or reject transactions before the payments clerk can execute the trade. Similarly, settlement instructions are managed by arranger/approver for setting up, modifying or changing standard SSIs. Protect and grow your corporate client franchise - Implement Caplin’s FX Corporate, and increase customer FX flows and loyalty by giving them: • Efficient treasury tools to effectively manage risks. • The ability to comply with the high levels of corporate governance. • The reassurance of using a strongly-branded product.

You can deliver a solution to your corporate customers in the shortest possible time using Caplin’s hosted e-distribution platform and FX Corporate solution. FX Corporate is also available as a Caplin Trader Motif, allowing you to customise and extend the functionality using the Caplin Trader framework and the Caplin Platform and integrate with your pricing and trading systems using the Caplin Integration Suite.

Who is it for?

For payments clerks who need to make FX transactions to cover foreign payments. For corporate treasurers who take a view on the market so they can manage their risk and cash flow with hedging.

Home Screen

The home screen is the entry point into the app and presents a set of streaming FX rates and a trade blotter showing executed trades. Streaming Rates

Streaming Rates

The streaming FX rates on the home screen give fast access to frequently used currency pairs. For each tile, the currency pair can be selected and tiles can be added and removed.

Trade Blotter

The trade blotter gives access to executed trades. Trade Details Full details of an executed trade can be accessed from the trade blotter. Blotter Filtering Using intuitive controls, the trade blotter can be filtered to show only the trades the user wants to see. Saved Blotters Filtered blotters can be saved to be viewed later or in other parts of the app. All blotters can be exported for use in other applications, such as Microsoft Excel.

Trading FX

A simple clean interface allows users to setup and execute Spot, Forward and Swap trades. Streaming Tradable Rate Tradable rates are streamed to the user. A count down is shown for the period the stream is valid. Trade Summary When a trade is executed a summary screen presents all the details of the trade back to the user. Swap Trades The simple interface extends to allow requesting and executing of FX Swaps trades. Supporting Information While setting up and executing a trade the right hand side of the app presents two trade blotters. The top blotter shows all executed trades and the lower blotter can be switched to a pre-defined or custom filtered view.

Time Options

The trade entry ticket also supports time options. A time option allows the user to specify an optional start date on a forward. Time Options Blotter The time options blotter provides access to all executed time options.

Strip of Forwards

A list of forward trades can be setup with different settlement dates and amounts. The strip can be priced and executed in one go. Strips also support time options. Generating Strips Using the smart generate tool, a strip of forwards can be quickly created with a series of settlement dates. Saving Strips User can save a strip of forwards for use later. This could either be to execute at a later date, or to save as a template for repeated use in the future.

Further down the road

Orders The ability to submit and manage single and complex orders. Notifications Notification users when orders are filled. Post Trade Post trade services such as allocations and approvals. Deal Management The ability to manage unsettled trades. Draw down time options and extend or pre-deliver forwards. Payments Integration with payments workflows.
