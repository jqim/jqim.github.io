Caplin Direct

A hosted, managed service for single-dealer platforms.

Caplin Direct is for banks that want a single-dealer platform delivered as complete service that includes delivery, support, and on-going maintenance.

Using Caplin Platform and Caplin Trader, the service connects to any existing e-trading systems and will enable a bank to offer a branded single-dealer platform to its clients.

Caplin Direct eliminates the technical challenges involved and reduces upfront costs, but will still allow unlimited branding, customization and extension with unique features and workflows. This approach reduces the need for IT involvement and helps the user to become independent. Caplin Direct benefits the IT department because they do not have to deploy, update, or otherwise maintain the solution, so that they are free to focus on value-added projects that contribute to their banks’ business goals.

What you get from Caplin Direct

  • Reduced cost: A fraction of the cost of an in-house build.
  • Reduced time to market: Bring your custom single-dealer platform to market in a matter of weeks rather than months.
  • Convenience: Bypass the internal barriers that can delay getting a single-dealer platform to market. Using Caplin Direct we can help you to build your single-dealer platform and then test, deploy and host it for you.
  • Focus: By choosing a managed service, you can focus on differentiating your online offering. You will have complete control over your workflows and can add as much pre or post trade functionality as you want.
  • Low risk: Caplin has demonstrated market-leading expertise in delivering secure e-distribution solutions to banks worldwide over the last ten years.
  • Reliability: Caplin has partnered with top financial hosting providers with unparalleled connectivity to your clients globally. Removing the challenges of complex multi-site resilient deployments, bandwidth and latency management
