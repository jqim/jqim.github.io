FX Sales

Caplin FX Sales

A solution that helps you deliver an outstanding trading experience to your FX sales team.

To build a scalable and cost-effective e-distribution business, banks are migrating clients from high- touch interaction using voice - to high-touch single- dealer platforms. Your sales people face challenges to more effectively manage an increasing number of clients. How do they keep track of individual client needs, monitor their trading activity, pro-actively manage upcoming rollover maturities, and demonstrate value to clients?

When quoting clients on the 'phone, it becomes harder for sales people to provide fast, optimal client pricing when they are unfamiliar with the client and the pre-defined margins. This creates greater challenges in meeting the twin objectives of sales profitability, whilst complying with regulatory processes and controls around ‘fair pricing/sales mark-ups’ for clients. Banks therefore need to invest in systems that will enable sales people to more effectively manage their clients, whilst ensuring they can capture sales-client interactions and identify,

In the past few years there has been a tremendous surge in the rate of growth for mobile technologies. During that time, the amount of internet traffic accessed via mobile devices has increased by a factor of ten. It is undeniable that the mobile trend is continuing to grow and is now spilling into the capital markets, where global and regional banks are now seeking to accommodate client demands for mobile trading solutions that offer real-time pricing and execution services.

Caplin’s FX Sales solution addresses challenges faced by sales people and senior management. It provides tools to increase productivty, whilst providing management with the oversight needed to capture, monitor and enforce appropriate sales mark-ups of client trades.

Configurable sales dashboards provide overviews of client trading activity, positions and trading limits. They can also display upcoming maturities and key client metrics. Active client profiles can be saved as ‘favourites’, whilst advanced search tools make it easy to identify a client and quickly access metrics needed to service in-bound client calls.

‘Trading on-behalf of’ functionality enables sales people to quote and execute trades on behalf of clients, with tickets pre-populated with default client spreads. Sales can lock dealing rates (resulting in variable margin), modify and then lock the applied margin (resulting in variable all-in client rate). Or they can enter the value of the sales mark-up (‘make me x$’) all within pre-defined management limits, thus ensuring clients are quoted appropriate mark-ups for trades done by phone.

Full metrics are captured for all sales-client interactions, providing management with record of actual margins, with the ability to set soft/hard maximum margins, ensuring fair and appropriate client pricing.

Increase your productivity - Implement Caplin’s FX Sales, and your sales people will be:

• more efficient due to optimised workflows • able to make better, more profitable deals • easier to track, monitor and oversee ...all whilst increasing revenue and lowering overheads

Who is it for?

Provide your FX sales team with complete control over client trades and put client information at their finger tips.

What can you do on FX Sales?

Client Selection

Gain quick access to clients and users using intuitive search facilities.

Trading for Clients

Trade on behalf of clients with full control over rates and margins.

Decision Support

Supportive information for the sales user when making trading decisions.

Sales Dashboard

The Sales Dashboard is the initial landing page that a sales user logging in to the application will see. This will present information useful to sales users to help them start their day effectively.

Overview The overview screen provides the sales user with useful information including system messages, daily news and reports. FX Rates Streaming FX rates in a space efficient design gives the sales user an instant view of the market for the most common currency pairs. Proactive Information to aid the sales user in proactively calling clients. This may include information on large deals close to maturity, orders close to the market or large orders recently filled. The sales dashboard is the main entry point in the application. This allows the sales user to see system messages, news and FX rates along side client information such as trades and orders. The components can be tailored to suit the individual.

Client Search

The client and user search panel is accessible from all parts of the application and gives quick access to recent clients and users as well as providing an intuitive search facility.

Client Dashboard

The Client Dashboard puts all the relevant information about the selected client at the finger tips of the sales user.

Client Information Contact and credit details along with any miscellaneous information or sales user notes about the selected client. Client Rates Streaming rates for the currency pairs used recently and most frequently by the selected client. The rates will include the default spread for the client. Blotters and Logs Filterable trade history and activity logs for this client either in full screen or smaller component form. The main client dashboard displays information about the client, including streaming rates that can be clicked to launch the trading workflow. Different components can be placed on the right hand side to suit the sales user.

Trading for Clients

The main function of the Caplin FX Sales is to allow sales users to trade on behalf of their clients. This needs to be an efficient workflow providing all the relevant information to the sales user.

Currency Pair Selection Fast and easy currency pair selection with access to recently traded and most frequently traded currency pairs. Spot, Forward & Swaps Trade outright spot and forwards and also even and uneven swaps. Control Margins Full control over margins and client rates including spot rate, forward points, all-in rate, near and far legs. Lock Client Rate The ability to lock the rate while discussing with the client. Live Profit Indicator A visual indication of profit that updates as the underlying trader rate changes. Reset Margin Easily reset margins back to the starting point to recover profit that have eroded as the trader rate changes. Trading for a Client The trading screen focuses on the main activity, giving plenty of room to the specifics of adjusting margins for spots, forwards and swaps. On the right hand side different components can be placed to add further information to help the process of executing the trade. Adjusting Margins The sales user has full control to adjust the margin on the spot rate and the forward points. The client rate will adjust accordingly as the margin is changed and as the trader rate ticks. Lock Client Rate Once the margins have been set the client rate can be locked. This allows the client rate (spot, points or all-in) to be adjusted without them being affected by the live ticking trader rate. Once the client rate is locked the trade button is enabled and the profit indicator becomes live. Live Profit Indicator With the client rate locked, the trade button and the profit indicator give clear visual information about the sales profit for the pending trade. As the trader rate ticks the sales profit is shown by the profit bar. If the profit drops below the default margin or drops below zero then the trade button highlights as red. Multiple Trades It is possible to add more trades to the screen and work on them concurrently. This allows sales users to setup multiple trades confirm at the same time with the client.

Trade Decision Support

Information relevant to the selected client and the currency pair being traded helps the sales user to make decisions on adjusting margins.

Activity Log A log of all activity relating to the client and the currency pair being traded. This includes trades, missed trades and sales notes. Chart / Highs / Lows A quick view of the days movement for the currency pair being traded. Trades and Orders A list of all trades and working orders for the selected client.

Further down the road

Orders The ability to submit and manage orders on behalf of clients. CRM Integration with Customer Relationship Management systems. Metrics Metrics and trends around client usage, including hit rates and other client behavior. Sales Intervention Allowing sales users to pick up trades that have failed a credit check and provide a rate for the client. Spreading Control Advanced control to setup the default spreads for clients. This includes sales users being able to influence the default margins rather than just changing margins on each trade.
