FX Mobile

 

Caplin FX Mobile

 A solution that helps you deliver an outstanding FX trading experience to mobile users.

People want access to the same information on t heir mobile device as they have on their desktop computer. By providing a mobile solution to your customers, they can be in touch with you wherever they are. You can deliver a branded mobile FX trading solution to your users with Caplin FX Mobile.

In the past few years there has been a tremendous surge in the rate of growth for mobile technologies. During that time, the amount of internet traffic accessed via mobile devices has increased by a factor of ten. It is undeniable that the mobile trend is continuing to grow and is now spilling into the capital markets, where global and regional banks are now seeking to accommodate client demands for mobile trading solutions that offer real-time pricing and execution services.

Our solution lets you... • Provide corporate clients with the ability to manage their risk and exposure. • Give professional trading clients the ability to trade whenever and wherever. • Enable internal traders to keep up to date with the latest rates and manage their positions when away from their desk. The design • Android and iOS support, deployed as a native, or as a web app. • Rapid, user-intuitive navigation makes trading on the move an enjoyable experience. • Responsive design to suit a variety of mobile device sizes such as Smartphones and Tablets. • Effortless to apply custom styling and logos to match your brand. What can you offer your customers? • Monitor, manage and submit orders with the flexibility to cancel, suspend and reinstate. • Execute Spot and Forward trades against streaming rates and view all executed trades. • Set up custom watch lists and keep up to date with the FX market • Granular permissioning, with ability to restrict users to read only access, simple order management or execution capabilities.

 

Who is it for?

Provide corporate clients with the ability to manage their risk and exposure.

Give professional trading clients the ability to trade whenever and wherever they are.

Allow your internal traders to keep up to date with the latest rates and manage their positions when away from their desk.

The Mobile Experience

The Caplin FX Mobile app is available on both iOS and Android devices. Smartphones and tablets are supported with a responsive design suited to the size of the device. Caplin FX Mobile can be deployed as a native app or as a web app.

Your App

Deliver your app to your users, with simple styling and branding.

What can you do on mobile?

Orders

 

Monitor, manage and submit orders while away from the desk or on the move.

Trades

View executed Spot, Forward and Swap trades and execute Spot and Forward trades against streaming rates.

Rates

Keep up to date with the FX market through custom watch lists with streaming rates.

Navigation

Home Screen

The home screen provides your users with fast access to all their data and keeps them up to date with the latest information. On the home screen and throughout the application, custom styling and logos can be applied so it matches your corporate brand.

In addition to the home screen navigation, a menu is available from all parts of the app. It gives users fast access to jump to other areas and allows them to configure which items are on the home screen.

The tablet and smartphone navigation are consistent, but tailored to each device.

Orders

Keeping up to date with orders is important for your users. With Caplin FX mobile you can provide users with access to all their orders in the system.

Monitor Orders

Users can access all their orders in a single list. Predefined filters are provided to focus on working orders, completed orders, suspended orders and cancelled orders. It is also possible to create custom filters to suit specific needs.

Complex Orders

The orders list supports displaying complex orders such as OCO, If Done and If Done OCO.

Managing Orders

The order details screen provides access to actions that can be performed on an order. Orders can be cancelled, suspended and reinstated from the device.

Submitting Orders

In addition to simple management of orders Caplin FX Mobile allows users to submit single limit orders.

Trades

 Caplin FX Mobile allows your users to view all their executed trades.

Trades

Executed trades can be accessed in a similar way to orders. Recent trades are listed, and a predefined filter for unsettled trades is available. Users can also create their own custom filters. The trade lists show summary information on executed Spot, Forward and Swap trades.

The trade details screen gives further information on trades, showing all the details of executed Spot, Forward and Swap trades.

Watchlists

Watchlists provide users with fast access to live rates and to keep in touch with the market wherever they are.

Manage Watchlists

You can provide users with predefined watchlists or they can create their own and easily add the currency pairs they are most interested in.

Executing Trades

 Users can access instrument details from their watch lists. From this screen they can also execute trades.Users can be permissioned for read only access, simple order management or for execution capabilities.

Notifications

 The app will notify users of events such as order fills and trade executions.

Financial news direct to your users devices.

Research
