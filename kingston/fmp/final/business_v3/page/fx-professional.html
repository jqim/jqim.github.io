FX Professional

FX Professional

Professional traders spend most of their time watching screens - at work, at home, or when out and about. They are always connected; monitoring rates, checking news, reading research/trade ideas, studying charts, awaiting economic figures, looking for the next opportunity. In their world, every tick counts, every move matters, and the performance, speed, reliability and portability of their trading platform is critical.

Such traders demand a broad range of trading styles, comprehensive order functionality and position management, highly responsive dealing screens and integrated pre-trade decision support tools including research, news and charting.

This creates opportunities for increased deal flows for banks; and especially regional banks. This because, with their unique view of regional liquidity, they are able to service this demanding client segment with decent pricing, low rejection rates and trader focused dealing screens. 

Caplin’s FX Professional solution enables you to meet the needs of the most demanding traders. It provides a complete turnkey solution comprising easily configurable components.

For example, you can create multi-functional trade tiles, with support for one/two click trading across spot, forwards and swaps. These could then be popped out to save valuable screen real- estate, or at a click can be extended to become volume forwards or swap ladders.

A comprehensive orders module covering single orders and strategies enables traders to more effectively manage their risk, execute their trading strategies and take advantage of market movements. Additionally, fully configurable and searchable trade, position and order blotters let them monitor and manage their positions and orders.

You can also keep traders up to date with the latest market moving news, trade ideas and technical charting with the suite of pre-trade decision tools. These provide seamless integration of research, trade ideas, and third-party news services, along with a real-time technical charting with studies. 

Increase your revenues - Implement Caplin’s FX Professional, and Professional traders will make more trades, more often with you, as they can:

• Access an FX trading interface that delights
• Trade with the confidence provided by low rejection rates • Rapidly react to market changes
• Efficiently manage risk
• Monitor the latest news and trends

...all in one place.

 

FX Professional in action.

The FX Motif is delivered with all the key elements you need to set up a robust FX trading front end. These elements are embodied as core features, and are available by default. Optional feature packs are also available as FX Motif upgrades.

Personalise the FX Motif trading environment with Webcentric facilities designed to give users a truly tailored experience. The environment comprises of workspace areas where tiles, grids and various other trading components can co-exist. Users can populate a workspace with selected trading components, and configure how those components appear onscreen. There are options to set up panes, create tabbed views, or add/remove components using convenient menu tools and search utilities. They can also rapidly view and manage multiple workspace areas, each containing a personalised set up of components and onscreen layouts. 

Caplin FX Professional includes blades that allow users to perform one-click trading on an executable streaming FX rate. The blades provide a rich set of functionality as standard and are easily extensible and customisable.

Alerting is a common requirement in trading apps. Event notifications implement an alert system that notifies users as trading events such as order fills and partial fills occur. A notification tray is provided to manage and view all event notifications.

Caplin FX Professional can display unread event notifications when users log in, and as they occur, with pop-ups for reviewing newly arrived notifications.

Set up and execute order tickets with the Orders Feature Pack. This pack provides a host of functions to set time in force, time to trade, prices and rates, actions for partial fills and much more. 

The Block Trading feature pack puts you in control of pricing, greatly reducing the spread as you trade large assortments of deals. Set up as many block trades (transactions containing several trade legs) as you like, pertaining to a large fund or deal, and even carry out pre-trade allocations.
