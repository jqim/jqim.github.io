Solutions overview

Solutions Overview

Single-dealer platforms have become an indispensable channel to market for any bank offering electronic trading services. The Caplin solution is highly scalable and performant; it uses best of breed technologies at each level - where they already existed we used, enhanced or extended them, and where they didn't exist, we've built them.

The Caplin Motifs are executable front-end interfaces with an elegant user experience that you can integrate with your existing trading systems. It's equipped with all the essential functionality you need to deliver trading execution functionality.

Caplin solutions are based on the best ideas from the most successful trading GUIs – ready-to-go, right out of the box. They are infinitely customisable, allowing you to create exactly the functionality and user experience that you require. By having suite of pre-built solutions it reduces the overall time to market for an in-house built single-dealer platform from 1-2 years to 3-4 months, and reduces the overall cost by 75% or more.

The Caplin FX Motif is built entirely using HTML5 and is provided as source code so that you can change, rewrite or extend any of it. It is easy to embed as a seamless part of a multi-asset offering. Our Professional Services team is always available to work with you to specify, project manage and develop your own unique trading app.
