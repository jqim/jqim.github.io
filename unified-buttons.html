Primary button

There should be only one of these given a choice of buttons, it should be the affermative action i.e. takes the user to the next step

Secondary button

These are ancillary choices to the primary action, or persistent functions

Small buttons

These are for functions that are localised to a portion of the screen. They can also be used to save space.

Form buttons

These buttons are deisnged to fit in with form fields.

Radial group

Like tabs or a group of radio buttons

Top button group

A group of buttons that control parameters for the content below it.

Bottom button group

A group of buttons are normaly aligned to the right to signify the next step in an interaction. The primary button is always on the far right
